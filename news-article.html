47th Philippine Association of Water Districts Convention Day 2

March 1, 2026 · By BCWD Public Relations

ADVISORY: Reduction of Water Supply

March 9, 2026 at 10:30 AM. Affected areas: Barangay 1–10.

Read more

The Butuan City Water District (BCWD) actively participated in the 47th Annual Convention of the Philippine Association of Water Districts (PAWD), held at the SMX Convention Center in Davao City. Day 2 of the convention featured a series of technical sessions and workshops focused on sustainable water management, climate change adaptation, and digital transformation in the water sector.

BCWD representatives presented the district's ongoing initiatives, including the Adopt-a-Forest program at the Taguibo River Watershed Forest Reserve and the AWAS (Automated Water Account System) digital platform that allows concessionaires to manage their accounts online.

Key Highlights

The convention brought together water district managers, engineers, and administrators from across the country to share best practices and discuss policy recommendations for the water sector.

  • Technical session on Non-Revenue Water reduction strategies
  • Workshop on climate-resilient infrastructure for water utilities
  • Presentation of the Breakwater publication award: Back 2 Back Best Magazine
  • Networking session with LWUA officials and partner agencies

BCWD General Manager Engr. John Michael Y. Benito expressed pride in the district's recognition and reaffirmed the commitment to providing safe, affordable, and sustainable water service to all concessionaires in Butuan City.
