About the Transparency Seal

The Transparency Seal is a certification issued by the Department of Budget and Management to government agencies that have satisfactorily complied with transparency requirements. BCWD displays this seal to demonstrate its commitment to open governance and public accountability.

As of reporting year 2025, BCWD has satisfied all requirements under Section 93 of the General Appropriations Act, including the posting of mandated transparency documents on its official website.
