How to Compute Your Bill

  1. Find your present reading on your water meter.
  2. Find your previous reading from your last bill.
  3. Subtract: Present − Previous = Cu. M Consumed.
  4. Enter that number in the Cu. M Consumed field.
  5. Click Compute Bill to see your estimated amount.
